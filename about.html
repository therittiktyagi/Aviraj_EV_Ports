About AEP

Our Legacy, Our Vision

From 17 years of infrastructure excellence to the forefront of India's EV revolution, AEP is driven by innovation, quality, and a commitment to sustainable mobility.

Our Story

From infrastructure pioneer to EV leader

Aviraj EV Ports Limited was born from a strong financial foundation and deep experience in India's infrastructure sector. With 17+ years of expertise in building reliable, scalable systems, we saw the future of mobility—electric, clean, and Made in India.

Today, AEP manufactures industry-standard AC and DC chargers, operates a 20+ city service network, and partners with CPOs, dealers, and fleet operators nationwide to accelerate India's sustainable mobility transition.

Our Mission

To establish India as a global leader in eco-friendly transportation solutions through cutting-edge EV charging infrastructure designed, manufactured, and deployed with indigenous excellence and global ambitions.

Our Values

  • 🌱 Sustainability – Every product and decision prioritizes environmental impact.
  • 🔧 Quality – Uncompromising standards in design, manufacturing, and service.
  • 🇮🇳 Make in India – Indigenous innovation and local capacity building.
  • 🤝 Partnership – Collaborative growth with CPOs, dealers, and stakeholders.

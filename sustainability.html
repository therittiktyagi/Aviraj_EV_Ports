COP26 Panchamrit Pledge

India's five elements for climate action

At COP26, Prime Minister Modi presented the Panchamrit—five nectar elements representing India's commitment to combat climate change. Sustainable mobility is a linchpin in this pledge, and EV charging infrastructure is the catalyst.

Element 1

Renewable Energy

AEP chargers integrate with solar, wind, and grid-scale renewables to deliver clean charging to EVs nationwide.

Element 2

Energy Efficiency

95%+ efficient DC chargers and optimized power management reduce energy losses compared to traditional fuel.

Element 3

Zero Emissions

EVs powered by AEP chargers eliminate tailpipe emissions, improving air quality in Indian cities and towns.

Element 4

Local Manufacturing

AEP chargers are made in India (Delhi, Noida), reducing carbon footprint from imports and building local capability.

Element 5

Jobs & Growth

AEP's growth creates skilled manufacturing, service, and technology jobs across 20+ Indian cities.

Impact

Global Leadership

Positioning India as a clean mobility innovator in alignment with global climate commitments.

FAQs

Frequently asked questions

What is the warranty on AEP chargers? +
AEP chargers come with a comprehensive 2-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Extended warranty options are available. Service support is available 24/7 across our 20+ service centers.
How long does installation take? +
Installation time varies based on charger type and site conditions. Typical AC charger installation: 2–4 hours. DC charger: 1–2 days. AEP handles site assessment, permitting, and commissioning.
Can I integrate AEP chargers with my existing software? +
Yes. AEP chargers are fully OCPP v1.6 compatible and can integrate with third-party management platforms via API. Our team can assist with custom integrations and white-label deployments.
What about maintenance and support? +
AEP provides 24/7 remote monitoring and diagnostics. Preventive maintenance is recommended annually. Our field service teams are available for repairs and troubleshooting at service centers nationwide.

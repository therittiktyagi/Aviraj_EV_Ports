Platform Architecture

One system, two perfect interfaces

AEP's platform connects EV drivers with CPOs (Charging Point Operators) through a centralized OCPP network with role-specific apps and full operational control.

For EV Users

Mobile App (Android & iOS)

Find, authenticate, and charge your vehicle on any connected AEP charger. Manage payments, view history, and get real-time notifications.

  • QR / RFID / OTP Authentication – Multiple secure login options
  • Charger Discovery & Filters – Location, power, and amenity-based search
  • Start / Stop Charging – Single-tap control with live status
  • Auto-Invoicing & Payments – Built-in billing with multiple payment modes
  • Profile Management – Save vehicles, payment methods, and preferences
  • In-App Notifications – Charging progress, offers, and support alerts
  • Help & Support – Direct access to AEP support team

For CPOs (Station Operators)

Centralized Web Console

Complete operational control: onboard chargers, manage tariffs, monitor performance in real-time, and optimize revenue across your network.

  • Charger Onboarding – Fast setup and registration of new stations
  • Station Listing with Amenities – Rich location details and facility info
  • Tariff Management – Dynamic pricing, peak/off-peak rates, discounts
  • Remote Operations & Control – Start/stop, reboot, and manage chargers remotely
  • Analytics Dashboard – Energy consumed, revenue, footfall, session data
  • Payment Settlement – Automated billing and reconciliation
  • Firmware Management – Over-the-air updates and device maintenance
